茶館

Chakan

Nestled within the elegant confines of the Sakura Hotel, Chakan offers a serene escape from the hustle and bustle of the city. As you step into the lobby, the inviting aroma of freshly brewed teas leads you to the tranquil oasis of Chakan. The tea house exudes an air of refinement, with traditional Japanese décor and panoramic views of the hotel's lush gardens. Guests are invited to indulge in a sophisticated high tea experience, featuring an exquisite selection of teas and meticulously crafted Japanese sweets. Whether you're seeking a quiet afternoon retreat or an elegant gathering with friends, Chakan promises to elevate your tea-drinking experience to new heights of luxury and sophistication.

Hizure

Located on the ground floor of the Sakura Hotel, Hiruze offers weary travelers and discerning diners alike a welcome respite from their daily adventures. The restaurant's warm and inviting ambiance, coupled with its understated elegance, sets the stage for a memorable dining experience. With an emphasis on fresh, locally sourced ingredients, Hiruze's menu features a diverse array of Japanese culinary delights, from hearty bowls of ramen to delicate sushi rolls. Whether you're stopping by for a quick bite or leisurely lunch, Hiruze invites you to savor the flavors of Japan in a relaxed and welcoming setting.

Banen

As night falls over the city, guests of the Sakura Hotel are invited to embark on a culinary journey of unparalleled sophistication at Banen.
