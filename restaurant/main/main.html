茶館 Chakan

Chakan offers a serene escape from the hustle and bustle of the city. The tea house exudes an air of refinement, with traditional Japanese décor and panoramic views of the hotel's lush gardens. Guests are invited to indulge in a sophisticated high tea experience, featuring an exquisite selection of teas and meticulously crafted Japanese sweets. Whether you're seeking a quiet afternoon retreat or an elegant gathering with friends, Chakan promises to elevate your tea-drinking experience to new heights of luxury and sophistication.
